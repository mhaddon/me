Chaos Engineering Video Game

My 'Corona project' was building a fun little 'Beat Em Up' video game for Chaos Engineering.

Chaos Engineering is a type of fault tolerance testing to ensure your organisation is ready for even the most unpredictable scenarios.

In this case, the idea was to randomly destroy and impact resources, services and servers inside of a Kubernetes cluster.

I was playing around with the idea that different teams in an organisation could 'Fight' each-other to see who made the most CloudNative and robust implementation.

Unfortunately, I never quite finished it, but maybe as the winter months return I might find some time.
